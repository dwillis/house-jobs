MEM-125-21

Congresswoman Grace Meng’s (NY-06) D.C. Office is seeking interns for the
upcoming Summer 2021 Semester.
In the Washington, DC office, internships run throughout the fall, spring or
summer semesters for college students. The hours are flexible to accommodate
